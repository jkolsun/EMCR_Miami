FAQ

Common Questions

What are the rental requirements?

Renters must be at least 21 years old with a valid driver's license. A security deposit and proof of insurance are required. Additional requirements may apply for certain high-value vehicles.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes! We offer complimentary delivery within Miami. We can deliver to airports, hotels, residences, or any location of your choice. Delivery typically takes under one hour.

What insurance options are available?

We offer comprehensive coverage options. You may also use your own auto insurance if it covers rental vehicles. Our team can help you understand your options during booking.

How does the deposit work?

A security deposit is required at the time of rental. The amount varies by vehicle. Deposits are released within 24 hours after the vehicle is returned and inspected.

Can I extend my rental?

Yes, extensions are available based on vehicle availability. Simply contact us before your rental ends and we'll do our best to accommodate your request.

What's your cancellation policy?

We offer flexible cancellation. Cancel at least 24 hours before your rental for a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours may be subject to a fee.
